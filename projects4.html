Hybrid Car Development

GreenPulse has partnered with Mercedes-AMG to develop a new generation of high-performance hybrid cars that combine power with sustainability. This collaboration aims to reduce carbon emissions while maintaining AMG’s signature engineering excellence. GreenPulse will provide environmental expertise and promote eco-friendly production methods. Meanwhile, Mercedes-AMG will focus on creating advanced hybrid systems that maximize energy efficiency and performance. Together, they seek to prove that speed and sustainability can coexist in modern mobility. This partnership marks a major step toward a cleaner and more responsible automotive future.
