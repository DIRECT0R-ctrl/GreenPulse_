Nuclear Energy

GreenPulse has joined forces with the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) to promote sustainable solutions for environmental protection. This collaboration focuses on using nuclear science to monitor pollution, manage water resources, and support clean energy initiatives. GreenPulse will lead local projects that apply these technologies to real ecological challenges. The IAEA will provide scientific expertise, training, and advanced tools to ensure effectiveness and safety. Together, they aim to reduce environmental degradation and encourage responsible resource management. This partnership represents a modern, science-based approach to ameliorating the environment worldwide.
