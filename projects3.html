Tree Planting

 GreenPulse has partnered with **Tree Nation** to launch a large-scale reforestation campaign aimed at restoring natural ecosystems. Together, they plan to plant thousands of trees across deforested and polluted areas to absorb CO₂ and protect biodiversity. GreenPulse will coordinate local volunteers and awareness events to engage communities in the movement. Tree Nation will provide expertise in tree planting, tracking, and long-term ecosystem management. This collaboration not only helps fight climate change but also improves air quality and soil health. Through this partnership, both organizations reaffirm their mission to **ameliorate the environment** sustainably.
